🔍 Why use a Center Punch?
As shown in the video, drill bits are designed to cut, but their tips can often "skate" or "wander" across smooth metal like our 1x2 aluminum tubes. 

1. **The Problem:** Without a punch, your hole might end up 1/16" or 1/8" away from your intended mark, ruining your alignment with the 3D printed Jig.
2. **The Solution:** The automatic center punch uses an internal spring mechanism to strike a hardened point into the metal. This creates a small crater that "traps" the tip of the drill bit, forcing it to stay perfectly on target.

!!! tip "Alignment Hack"
    Line the tip of the punch up exactly where your scribed layout lines cross. Press down firmly until you hear the "click"—that indicates the internal hammer has fired.
