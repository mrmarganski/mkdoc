Students will show an understanding of tools used and demonstrate their understanding...


* Students have to pick correct tool for each operation
* 3D Print a shape (we can have them design the shape or give them it)  The 3D Print has a hole pattern of 4/5 holes - 10/32 and maybe a tapped hole insert in the print.  It will act as the "JIG/Fixture"
* Students cut a length of material (1x2 tube) - have to measure and cut straight (no milling allowed) and fit in the "jig"
* They also have to measure the holes on the 3d print part, and drill with hand drill the 4/5 holes into the tube so that the correct fastener attached them in the correct spot. (Not while in the jig)
