# 🟡 Level 2: Power Tools & Basics Overview
> **Objective:** Transition from hand tools to electrically powered equipment, including handheld power tools, benchtop machinery, and 3D printers, with a strict focus on operational safety.

Welcome to Level 2 Fabrication. You have proven you know how to measure twice, turn a wrench, and respect the workspace in Level 1. Now, we add electricity. 

In this level, the tools do the heavy lifting. Because these machines spin at high speeds and generate intense heat, the safety requirements in the shop are significantly elevated. 

!!! warning "Strict PPE Rules (Personal Protective Equipment)"
    Before touching anything in Level 2, you must ensure your safety glasses are on, long hair is securely tied back, hoodie strings are tucked in, and baggy sleeves are rolled up. **Rotating machinery does not forgive.**

---

## ⚡ What You Will Learn in Level 2

By the end of this level, you will be cleared to operate the fundamental powered equipment of our shop:

* 🔋 **Drills & Impacts:** How to properly chuck a drill bit, drill straight holes, and use an impact driver without stripping screw heads or snapping bits.
* 🗜️ **Benchtop Machinery:** Safe operation of the Drill Press, Bench Grinder, and Belt Sander. You will learn how to secure your workpieces tightly so they don't become spinning projectiles.
* 🖨️ **FDM 3D Printing:** How to slice files, load filament, and operate the Bambu Lab X1C and Flashforge printers to create custom plastic parts.
* 🔌 **Soldering & Electronics:** Safely using a soldering iron to splice wires, tin leads, and make secure electrical connections without burning yourself or the workbench.
* 📝 **Skill Check - Level 2:** A required assessment to prove you know your hand power tools before moving to stationary power tools.

---

## 🔒 Looking Ahead: The Advanced Pathway

Completing Level 2 means you have officially passed your Phase 1 Rookie Fabrication requirements! You will be fully cleared to help build and assemble the competition robot. 

Once you pass this level, you will unlock **Phase 2: Advanced Fabrication (Levels 3 & 4)**, which introduces heavy stationary equipment like the **Band Saw**, **Table Saw**, and our Level 4 for our computer-controlled CNC/Laser machines.
