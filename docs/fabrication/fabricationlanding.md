# 🛠️ Shop & Fabrication Pathways
> Master the machines that build our robots. Learn proper techniques, maintenance, and operation.

!!!danger
    Our workspace is not a home garage. We follow strict, standardized methods to ensure safety and best practices. These protocols are mandatory for everyone, regardless of prior experience, confidence, or lack thereof.  Embracing the FIRST Core Values of Inclusion and Teamwork, the Panther Project supports all members by meeting you exactly where your comfort zone is. Whether you are a veteran builder or using a tool for the first time, our priority is safe, collaborative discovery. We build each other up as we build the robot.
