# Welcome to FRC Robotics - Panther Project!

Welcome to the team! This guide is for **ALL** members of our FRC robotics club.  Whether you are new and it is you are still asking the question, "What does the 2064 stand for?" or a veteran member who is asking questions like, "What does the "P" in PID stand for?" We have material to help you build on your exisiting skills and find a way to support this team!  You will find Engineering skills, JAVA programming guides, and Non-Engineering related content that altogether are the ingredients of **SUCCESSFUL** team when we engage in these skills and build on them, while bringing other members along and supporting them throguh the process as well.
