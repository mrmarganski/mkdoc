# 🏆 Phase 3: Advanced Competition & Leadership
> **Prerequisite:** Veteran status, Subteam Lead role, or Mentor approval.

Building the robot is only half the battle. Phase 3 is reserved for veteran members, subteam leads, and the drive team. 

In this phase, you will zoom out from individual part creation to focus on macro-level team operations and event execution. This is where you learn how to read a game manual to find strategic loopholes, manage a high-stress pit crew during an 8-minute turnaround, and lead engineering design reviews. 

!!! info "Stepping into Leadership"
    Unlike Phases 1 and 2, Phase 3 modules are not strictly linear. You may be asked by a mentor to complete a specific module based on your leadership role for the season (e.g., the Scouting Lead will focus heavily on Pathway 3.1, while the Pit Boss will focus on 3.2).
